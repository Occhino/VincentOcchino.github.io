Educational Background

I went to pre-school at Fountain Valley United Methodist Church. Then for kindergarten through seventh grade I went to Miola. Afterwards, I attended Shoreline, a private Christian school, for eighth grade. Then I attended Fountain Valley High School, in Fountain Valley. At Fountain Valley High School, I ran cross-country and track. I ran the one and two-mile races in track and field. My junior year of high school I took a regional occupational program class that gave me hands on experience as a mechanic.

I graduated high school in June 2006. Subsequently, I attended Orange Coast College (OCC), a community college, before transferring to California State University Monterey Bay (CSUMB). I graduated OCC in Spring 2015 and I expect to graduate CSUMB in Spring 2018.

I have a background in accounting and finance. I received an entry level accounting certificate from OCC and at CSUMB my concentration is the financial analysis track of accounting.
